TrackIT

Our last sprint continued our recent theme of changes that allow our platform to be much more scalable. In particular, we are working on a redesign of our database schema which will not prioritize scalability and extensibility, allowing us to build new features and expand our database as needed. 

This sprint didn’t include any changes that would be noticeable to the end-user. However, these changes will allow us to add lots of new features in the near future such as allowing workshop hosts to provide file resources and allowing users to upload files for hosts to review.

Debut with ACM Education

  • TrackIT was successfully used for its first virtual workshop by the ACM Education team in partnership with SWE & AWS.
  • We had 30+ new users sign on to our application for the first time & are now onboarded through the ACM SSO.
  • Looking forward to being used by the team for many more events. 
Looking for additional developers to join the team to continue rolling out new features and keep up with the demand from external organizations!
